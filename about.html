About Orphan Care

Our journey to provide love, shelter and education to orphaned children around the world

Our Story

Founded in 2010 by a group of passionate volunteers, Orphan Care began as a small shelter in Nairobi, Kenya. What started as a home for 5 children has grown into an international organization helping thousands of orphans annually.

Our founder, Dr. Amina Wanjiru, was orphaned herself at age 7. Her personal experience drives our mission to ensure no child faces the challenges she endured alone.

Our Mission

To provide orphaned children with stable homes, quality education, and the love every child deserves.

Safe Shelter

We operate 12 homes across 3 countries, providing safe, loving environments.

Education

Every child in our care receives quality schooling and vocational training.

Family

We create family units where children experience love and belonging.
